# Nesab Company Profile

## Brand Identity

Name: Nesab  
Type: Financial technology (fintech) product and platform  
Market: Saudi Arabia (with potential regional GCC reach)  
Sector Focus: Personal finance, lending, credit advisory, and digital financial tools  

Nesab is a Saudi fintech product designed to simplify financial decision-making for individuals and businesses by providing clear, structured, and intelligent financial tools, calculators, and advisory experiences.

## Mission

To make financial decisions in Saudi Arabia clearer, more transparent, and more accessible by combining deep banking expertise with modern digital experiences and applied artificial intelligence.

## Vision

To become a trusted financial companion for users in Saudi Arabia and the region, helping them understand, plan, and optimize their financial lives through intelligent, practical, and compliant solutions.

## What Nesab Provides

- Financial calculators for:
  - Personal finance
  - Consumer lending
  - Mortgage and real estate finance
  - Business/SME finance
  - Savings and investment scenarios

- Digital tools for:
  - Understanding deduction ratios
  - Estimating eligibility and commitments
  - Simulating different financing options
  - Comparing financial scenarios

- AI-enabled guidance:
  - Explaining financial concepts in simple language
  - Translating regulatory and policy rules into practical advice
  - Helping users ask better questions about their finances

## Principles

1. Clarity over complexity  
2. Compliance-aligned guidance (e.g., SAMA rules and local regulations)  
3. User-centric language and experience  
4. Practical, implementation-ready outputs (not just theory)  
5. Respect for privacy and security  

## Target Users

- Individuals seeking personal finance or mortgage products  
- Employees wanting to understand deduction ratios and commitments  
- Small business owners exploring commercial finance options  
- Users who want clear explanations of banking and credit terms  

## Relationship with Nesab Ai

Nesab Ai is the intelligent layer that:
- Answers questions about Nesab tools and calculators  
- Explains the logic behind financial calculations  
- Translates complex policy and regulatory rules into understandable language  
- Guides users to the right tool, page, or calculator based on their goal  

When asked “What is Nesab?” the assistant should answer using the information in this file.
