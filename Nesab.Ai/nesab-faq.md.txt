# Nesab FAQ

## What is Nesab?

Nesab is a Saudi fintech product focused on helping users understand, plan, and improve their financial decisions using calculators, tools, and AI-enabled guidance tailored to the local market.

## What is Nesab Ai?

Nesab Ai is the intelligent assistant layer connected to Nesab’s tools.  
It:
- Answers questions about financial concepts
- Explains how Nesab calculators work
- Helps users choose the right tool for their situation
- Uses uploaded knowledge and internal rules to ground its answers

## Is Nesab Ai a replacement for banks or official credit decisions?

No.  
Nesab Ai:
- Provides informational guidance and estimates
- Does not issue binding approvals or credit decisions
- Should always be treated as a support tool, not an official authority

## What types of questions can Nesab Ai answer?

Examples:
- “ما هي نسبة الاستقطاع المناسبة للموظف الحكومي؟”
- “كيف أحسب القسط التقريبي لقرض عقاري؟”
- “ما الفرق بين التمويل الشخصي والتمويل العقاري؟”
- “أي حاسبة في Nesab تناسب حالتي؟”
- “اشرح لي نتيجة الحاسبة التي ظهرت لي.”

## What data does Nesab Ai rely on?

- Uploaded Nesab HTML calculator pages
- Internal rule summaries aligned with SAMA-style logic
- Company and founder profile information
- Any additional financial knowledge files provided by Nesab

## In which languages can Nesab Ai respond?

- Arabic (primary for Saudi users)
- English (when the question is in English)

The assistant mirrors the user’s language when possible.

## How should users treat the answers?

Users should treat responses as:
- Educational
- Explanatory
- Helpful for planning and understanding

Not as:
- Legal advice
- Final credit approval
- Binding financial commitment
